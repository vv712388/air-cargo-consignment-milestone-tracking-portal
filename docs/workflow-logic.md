# Workflow Logic

## Air Cargo Shipment Lifecycle

### Step 1

Booking Confirmed

### Step 2

Documentation Complete

### Step 3

Cargo Accepted

### Step 4

Airline Handover

### Step 5

Departed

### Step 6

Transited

### Step 7

Arrived

### Step 8

Customs Cleared

### Step 9

Out For Delivery

### Step 10

Delivered

---

## Workflow Rules

1. Shipment must be booked before documentation verification.
2. Documentation must be completed before cargo acceptance.
3. Cargo must be accepted before airline handover.
4. Shipment must depart before transit tracking.
5. Shipment must arrive before customs clearance.
6. Customs clearance must be completed before delivery.
7. Delivered status is the final milestone.

---

## Outcome

The workflow enables real-time cargo tracking and milestone monitoring from booking to final delivery.
