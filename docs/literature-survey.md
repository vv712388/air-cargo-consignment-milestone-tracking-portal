# Literature Survey

## Reference 1: DHL Tracking System

* Provides shipment tracking services.
* Shows shipment status updates.
* Offers customer visibility for cargo movement.
* Supports international shipment tracking.
* Limited air cargo operational dashboards.

## Reference 2: FedEx Tracking System

* Real-time shipment tracking.
* Customer notification features.
* Detailed shipment history.
* Supports global logistics operations.
* Limited air cargo milestone workflow.

## Reference 3: UPS Tracking System

* Package and cargo tracking.
* Shipment history management.
* Delivery notifications.
* Customer visibility features.
* Generic logistics focus.

## Summary

The reviewed systems provide tracking capabilities and shipment visibility. However, they primarily focus on general logistics operations. The proposed Air Cargo Consignment Milestone Tracking Portal will provide dedicated air cargo milestone tracking, operational dashboards, customs tracking, documentation management, and delivery visibility.
